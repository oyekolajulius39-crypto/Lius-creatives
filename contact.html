Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How fast can you deliver?

Most graphic design projects are delivered within 24-48 hours. Complex branding packages may take 3-5 days. We always discuss timelines before starting any project.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! We include 2-3 rounds of revisions with all our packages to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, online payments, and mobile money transfers. 50% deposit is required to start most projects, with the balance due on completion.

Can you work with businesses outside Lagos?

Absolutely! We work with clients all across Nigeria and even internationally. Everything is done remotely through WhatsApp, email, and video calls.
